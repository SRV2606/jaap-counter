Core Features

Designed for Absolute Simplicity

Everything you need for a consistent, meaningful spiritual practice — elegantly in one place.

🎙️

Hands-Free Voice Chanting

Simply chant aloud and our AI counts every Jaap automatically — no tapping, no distractions, just pure devotion. Or switch to Touch Mala for the traditional bead-counting feel.

🕉️

Daily Panchang

AI-powered daily Panchang with auspicious timings tailored to your birth details and city. Tithi, Nakshatra, Rahukaal and more — every single day.

✨

Rashi Horoscope

Beautifully crafted daily horoscopes and personalized Vedic remedies for your Rashi — staying connected to traditional Jyotish wherever you live.

📿

Vedic Mantra Library

20+ traditional mantras — Gayatri, Om Namah Shivaya, Mahamrityunjaya, Hare Krishna and more — with full Sanskrit text, Hindi meaning, and benefits.

✍️

Create Your Own Mantra

Personalize your practice. Type or speak any mantra and start chanting instantly. AI adds deeper meaning and intention to your personal practice.

📅

Hindu Calendar

Never miss a vrat, festival or sacred observance. A complete Hindu calendar with daily tithi details and upcoming events.

📊

Streak & Progress Tracking

Track your lifetime chant count, daily streak, and meditation time. Watch your devotion grow — one bead at a time.

🔒

100% Private

No login required. Your mantras, voice, and location data never leave your device. Your spiritual journey is yours alone.
