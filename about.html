About Jaap

Jaap – Mantra & Meditation is a calm and mindful digital companion designed to support focused mantra repetition and spiritual reflection.

Jaap helps users engage in mantra chanting using voice or touch-based interaction in a distraction-free environment. The app is built to encourage slow, intentional practice rather than performance or outcomes.

Jaap includes features such as

Voice-based mantra chanting
Touch-based mala counting
A curated library of sacred mantras
Personal mantra creation
Hindu calendar, Panchang, and horoscope guidance
Optional notifications for app communication

Jaap is intended to support personal spiritual routines and mindfulness practices. It does not claim to provide medical, psychological, or guaranteed results. The app does not offer predictive astrology, health advice, or religious instruction.

Jaap respects diverse spiritual paths and is designed for anyone seeking a calm and reflective mantra practice experience.
