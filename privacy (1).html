Privacy Policy

Daily App Studios built the Jaap – Mantra & Meditation app as a free application. This page is used to inform users regarding policies on the collection, use, and disclosure of information.

User privacy is a core value of Jaap. The app is designed to collect only minimal data required for functionality and improvement.

Information We Collect

Jaap may collect limited, non-personal information, including

Anonymous app interaction data (via Firebase Analytics)
Crash and error reports (via Firebase Crashlytics)
Device identifiers used for optional notifications (via Firebase Cloud Messaging)
Approximate location data, collected on-device and used solely for feature improvement — this data is not transmitted to our servers or shared with third parties

Jaap does not collect

Voice recordings
Spoken mantra text
Personal identity information
Contacts
Health or medical data

How We Use Information

Collected information is used solely to:

Improve app stability and performance
Understand feature usage patterns
Improve app features using on-device location context
Deliver optional app notifications
Display relevant advertisements

Third-Party Services

Jaap uses the following third-party services:

Firebase Analytics – for anonymous usage analytics
Firebase Crashlytics – for crash and error reporting
Firebase Cloud Messaging – for optional notifications
Google AdMob – for serving in-app advertisements
Google Generative AI – for powering Panchang, Rashi astrology, and Daily Hindu Calendar content

These services may collect information as described in their respective privacy policies.

Advertising

Jaap displays advertisements provided by Google AdMob. AdMob may use device identifiers and other non-personal data to serve contextually relevant ads. Jaap does not share personal data with advertisers. For more information on how Google uses data from apps that use its services, visit Google's Privacy & Terms.

AI-Powered Features

Jaap uses Google Generative AI to provide content for the following features:

Panchang – daily Hindu almanac information
Rashi – zodiac and astrology guidance
Daily Hindu Calendar – auspicious dates, tithi, and festival information

When you access these features, your query or request context (such as date, time, or selected preferences) may be sent to Google's Generative AI services to generate a response. No personal identity information, voice data, or sensitive data is included in these requests.

The content generated by Google Generative AI is provided for informational and spiritual reference purposes only. Jaap does not guarantee the accuracy of AI-generated astrological or calendar content, and it should not be relied upon as authoritative religious, predictive, or professional guidance. Use of this feature is subject to Google's Privacy Policy.

App Permissions

Jaap requests the following Android permissions to enable its features:

Notifications To send optional app updates and reminders, which can be disabled in device settings at any time
Location Used on-device to improve app features; not transmitted to our servers or shared with third parties
Microphone To enable voice-based mantra chanting; voice input is processed locally and not recorded or stored
Internet Required for analytics, crash reporting, ad delivery, and notification services

All permissions are used strictly for the purposes described above. You may revoke any permission at any time through your device's app settings.

Notifications

Jaap may send optional notifications related to app communication or updates. Notifications are entirely optional and can be disabled at any time through device settings.

Data Security

Jaap takes reasonable measures to protect user data. No sensitive or personal data is stored by the application. Location data is processed on-device only and is not transmitted to external servers.

Children's Privacy

Jaap is not directed at children under the age of 13. The app does not knowingly collect personal data from children.
