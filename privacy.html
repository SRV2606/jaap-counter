Privacy Policy

Daily App Studios built the Jaap – Mantra & Meditation app as a free application. This page informs users regarding our policies on the collection, use, and disclosure of information when using the app.

Protecting your privacy is a core value of Jaap. The app is designed to collect only the minimal data required for functionality, improvement, and advertising.

Information We Collect

Jaap may collect the following information

Anonymous app interaction and usage data (via Firebase Analytics)
Crash and error reports (via Firebase Crashlytics)
Device tokens used for optional push notifications (via Firebase Cloud Messaging)
Device advertising identifier, used by Google AdMob to serve relevant advertisements
Approximate location, collected on-device and used solely to improve app features such as the Hindu Calendar and Panchang. This data is not transmitted to or stored on our servers.
Voice audio, captured when you use the voice-based mantra chanting feature. Audio is sent to Google’s Speech Recognition service for transcription. Jaap does not store or retain any audio recordings or transcription results.

Jaap does not collect

Your name, email address, or any personal identity information
Contacts or address book data
Health or medical data
Payment or financial information

User-Created Content

When you create a custom mantra using the Create Mantra feature, the text and any associated intention you enter are stored locally on your device only. This content is not transmitted to our servers or shared with any third party, except when you choose to use the AI enrichment feature, in which case the mantra text may be sent to Google Generative AI to generate additional context.

How We Use Information

Collected information is used solely to:

Improve app stability and performance
Understand feature usage patterns to improve the app
Provide localised Hindu Calendar, Panchang, and auspicious date information using on-device location context
Enable voice-based mantra counting by transcribing your spoken chants
Deliver optional app notifications
Display relevant advertisements via Google AdMob

Third-Party Services

Jaap uses the following third-party services. Each may collect data as described in their respective privacy policies.

Firebase Analytics – anonymous usage analytics
Firebase Crashlytics – crash and error reporting
Firebase Cloud Messaging – optional push notifications
Google AdMob – in-app advertisements, including banner and rewarded video ads. AdMob may collect your device advertising identifier.
Google Speech Recognition – transcribes voice audio for the mantra chanting feature. Audio is processed by Google and is subject to Google’s privacy policy. Jaap does not retain any audio or transcription data.
Google Generative AI – powers Panchang, Rashi astrology, Daily Hindu Calendar content, and optional mantra enrichment

Advertising

Jaap displays advertisements provided by Google AdMob, including banner ads and rewarded video ads. Rewarded ads may be shown when you have reached the free limit for custom mantras; watching a rewarded ad allows you to continue using the feature.

AdMob may use your device advertising identifier and other non-personal data to serve contextually relevant ads. Jaap does not share personal data with advertisers. You can opt out of personalised advertising at any time through your device settings under Google › Ads.

For more information on how Google uses data from apps that use its services, visit Google’s Privacy & Terms.

Voice Recognition

The voice-based mantra chanting feature uses Android’s SpeechRecognizer, which is powered by Google’s Speech Recognition service. When you chant a mantra aloud, your voice audio is transmitted to Google’s servers for transcription. The resulting text is used only to match your chant against the selected mantra and increment your count.

Jaap does not record, store, or retain any audio or transcription data. The audio is processed transiently by Google and is subject to Google’s Privacy Policy.

AI-Powered Features

Jaap uses Google Generative AI to provide content for the following features:

Panchang – daily Hindu almanac information
Rashi – zodiac and astrology guidance
Daily Hindu Calendar – auspicious dates, tithi, and festival information
Custom Mantra Enrichment – optional additional context for mantras you create

When you access these features, your request context (such as date, time, selected preferences, or mantra text) may be sent to Google’s Generative AI services to generate a response. No personal identity information, retained audio data, or sensitive personal data is included in these requests.

Content generated by Google Generative AI is provided for informational and spiritual reference only. Jaap does not guarantee the accuracy of AI-generated content, and it should not be relied upon as authoritative religious, predictive, or professional guidance. Use of these features is subject to Google’s Privacy Policy.

App Permissions

Jaap requests the following Android permissions to enable its features:

Microphone To enable voice-based mantra chanting. Audio is sent to Google’s Speech Recognition service for transcription and is not stored by Jaap.
Notifications To send optional app updates and reminders. Can be disabled at any time in device settings.
Location Used on-device to provide localised Hindu Calendar and Panchang information. Location data is not transmitted to our servers or shared with third parties.
Vibration To provide haptic feedback during mantra chanting sessions, helping you maintain rhythm and awareness.
Internet Required for analytics, crash reporting, ad delivery, AI-powered features, voice recognition, and notification services.
Network State Used to check connectivity before making network requests, preventing unnecessary errors.

All permissions are used strictly for the purposes described above. You may revoke any permission at any time through your device’s app settings.

Notifications

Jaap may send optional notifications related to your practice, auspicious dates, or app updates. Notifications are entirely optional and can be disabled at any time through your device settings.

Data Security

Jaap takes reasonable measures to protect user data. No sensitive or personal data is stored by the application on our servers. Location data is processed on-device only. User-created content such as custom mantras is stored locally on your device.

Children’s Privacy

Jaap is not directed at children under the age of 13. The app does not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the app, please contact us and we will take steps to remove it.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
